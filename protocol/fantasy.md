## Cat box (Nelly, Marcel, Jenny)

- a cat box with video call and a laser pointer to play with the cat remotely
- petting is also remotely possible with a servo motor
- a hologram of the cat is the input device together with some buttons and a speaker for communication
- a small joystick is used to control the laser pointer
- Actors: cat, catbox, owner, hologram
